The Wakagoto Foundation aims to uplift **individuals, families**, and **communities** across **Embu County** and beyond. Some of the key groups it serves include:

- **Mango farmers in Kanyuambora** seeking sustainable markets for their produce.
- **Children at Rwagori Primary School** who deserve modern educational facilities.
- **Unemployed youth** across Embu County looking for economic empowerment.
- **Families in Mwea and Makima** needing access to clean, safe drinking water.
- **Chicken farmers in Kiritiri** aiming to expand into new markets.
- **Tea farmers in Mbuori Village** seeking dignified outlets for their produce.
- **Boda boda riders in Runyenjes** striving for a better working environment.

The foundation‚Äôs initiatives are designed to create opportunities, restore dignity, and transform lives in meaningful ways.
